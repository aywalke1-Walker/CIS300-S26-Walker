Common Ingredients & Their Benefits

Hyaluronic Acid

Helps keep skin hydrated and plump by holding moisture.

Salicylic Acid

Clears pores and helps reduce acne and oil buildup.

Niacinamide

Improves skin texture, reduces redness, and controls oil.

Retinol

Helps with anti-aging, fine lines, and skin renewal.

Vitamin C

Brightens skin and helps reduce dark spots.
